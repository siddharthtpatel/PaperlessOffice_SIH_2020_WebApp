Going paperless with E-Daftar

The idea is to take a step forward to sustainablity by going paperless. We build a paperless management system, E-Daftar, that is adaptable to every type of organization. Our flexible paperless management system gives organizations an option for a variable workflow. The prototype supports conversion of scanned documents to e-Document with auto-validation checks. The portal is available on Web as well as on the Mobile App for easiness. Updates and notifications are directly synced to the user’s phone number and mobile app. We encourages all organizations to adapt a paperless system with E-Daftar.
